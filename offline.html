Offline Charging

"If ever your phone is dead -- empty battery:"

NOTE: This is 1 peso for 3 minutes charging only, It will automatically STOP
if you wish to continue, you can avail Chargify for its monitored time remaining feature.

Step 1: Use the coinslot on your right, facing the vending machine.

Step 2: Plug your cellphone to the matched cable cord near the right-side coin slot.

Step 3: Insert 1 pesos coin.

Step 4: After a few seconds, Power On your cellphone to reboot your system.
